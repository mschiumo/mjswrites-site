i wrote some of my best lines for you,  
and now, they’re all that remains  
how sorrowful and tragic,  
to lose someone who had once inspired me so  
perhaps, i am clouded by nostalgia,  
the whimsical belief that things in the past had somehow been better…  
but they were good with you  
they were – simple  
a grounding in a universe bent on throwing us all astray  
but you became the calm  
the reason to look forward to evenings  
the reminder that romance lives on, so long as we search for it  
and now, the lines i wrote for you have been enjoyed by people from across the globe  
leaving them with what i regret to have lost – the best of you

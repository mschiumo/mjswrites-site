there’s a warmth that accompanies the memory of you  
not because you belong to the past, but simply because you’ve danced across my mind  
it’s the pleasant moment that follows when I recall your smile, or perhaps hear your laugh reverberate when no one else can  
it drives me to ensure that I hear it again, that I find a way to bring you joy when nothing else seems capable  
just for the chance to be amidst your warmth

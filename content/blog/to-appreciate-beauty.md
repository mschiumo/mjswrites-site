it has been but hours since you were no more than a stranger  
and now, you're the one i crave most  
you have to appreciate beauty when you see it,  
and my words are the purest form i can conceive to convey to you how i study your face  
how entranced and gripped i am by your gaze  
how the moments between the current and when i see you next will be agonizing  
so, when i first saw you,  
i knew that it would be many nights hence devoid of sleep,  
but replete with the thought of you  
and that's just fine with me

of all there is to do in this world,  
nothing could surpass my desire to simply write  
to challenge myself to encase the entirety of my experiences in flowery words and clever lines  
to find meaning when it has done its best to remain obscure  
these words will always be my guiding light, there to uncover the subtleties  
when i am gone, i hope that they will linger a while longer  
to steward my best intentions when i cannot  
for, as i had once attested, all of my heroes are poets  
and it is to their legacy that i aspire  
to find words that will not only endure, but lift others  
only then shall i be guarded from death  
only then shall this desire melt away  
until that day, i wish only that neither my pen nor my inspiration run dry  
for there are many more clever lines to write  
on this path i’ve chosen

even when i wake,  
reality meets me kindly, and the surreal has replaced the mundane  
it is a reminder to live to the fullest,  
to be grateful for the privilege of experiencing what once felt far-fetched  
i shall hold onto this feeling long into the twilight,  
lest it slip away forever

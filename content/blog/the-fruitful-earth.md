another year full of challenges and victory,  
how blessed to live through yet another revolution of this fruitful earth  
as long as my faith endures,  
i am not alone  
my heart beats with my kin,  
and i find life precious most when reflecting upon it  
i will set my gaze on distant shores,  
knowing that i will reach them in due time,  
and, until then, my only desire is to keep living

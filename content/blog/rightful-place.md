the softness of your features is inviting  
how your eyes ignite, effusive and intoxicating  
between us, words come without difficulty  
as though we had just begun again where we left off in a previous life  
your voice is nothing short of enchanting, a rich mixture of intrigue and softness,  
so delicate and sincere, rendering time immaterial  
we speak of our lives as though they had always been intertwined  
two familiar souls reuniting  
two journeys coalescing  
two spirits together again  
and I can hardly endure the wait  
to find you beside me once more

all that i own fits into a suitcase,  
but this is what i have craved for so long  
a life unfettered by possession,  
the choice to consume less, and experience more  
the ability to smother this incessant need for exorbitance  
alas, the things i value most could never fit in a suitcase...  
my ambitions,  
my dreams,  
my thirst for all that the world has to offer  
this simple actualization of what had long been a wish has been fulfilled,  
and although i harbor fears for the materialistic future,  
i cherish the intangible i have been given  
knowing that it is many a rich man who would give all that they have  
to cast it all away and live a happy life  
from a suitcase

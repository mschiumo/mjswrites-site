i can't be the only one who lives and dies with the tension between us  
my gaze lingers on you, and, every so often, it seems that my dream of being yours is within reach  
when will you seize it, and take your chance?  
it is the uncertainty that is unbearable,  
but i remain patient,  
waiting for the day that you call me yours for the first time

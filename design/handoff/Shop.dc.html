a poem for two

Between Two

❦
I find something so captivating in your eyes
Or, maybe, it has found me
So, I'll linger here for a few seconds more
As the world bustles along, as it always has
And revel in the joy of a new beginning
Content to be still as I uncover more of you
I'll chance one last glance
As you drift silently away
Setting my gaze on the dwindling twilight
Grateful for this moment
Shared between two

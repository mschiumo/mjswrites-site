from the day you first met  
to the day you vowed never to part  
i’ve borne witness to your love story  
finding joy and immeasurable hope within it  
grateful to have seen the beauty of a blossoming love  
now, your affections have coalesced into new life  
and a new love that shall know no bounds  
how much we shall cherish him…  
i can only give you my best wishes  
that your dreams continue to become reality  
and what greater blessing than the promise of a future  
stewarded by two who have become one?  
two who choose each day  
to continue writing the next chapters of this love story  
that will be told for generations to come

i’m not empty, just perpetually filled with sorrow  
what is there to inspire peace when my existence is beset by misery?  
pain washes over me in merciless waves  
until even the joy of others feels foreign  
it seems they will never quite understand why it is that living is so arduous for me  
or why it feels as though i can never truly be free until the injustices of the world have been rectified

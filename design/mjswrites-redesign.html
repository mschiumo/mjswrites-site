(In)Sane — Memoirs of a Manic Millennial

An expedition through the depths of a mind lost in mania. After years of undiagnosed Bipolar, I found myself at the precipice of disaster — jobless, broke, and alone in a psych ward. The tale begins between hospitalizations, still embroiled in severe mania.

Told in two parts capturing the full spectrum of Bipolar's moods, (In)Sane is a brutally honest account of uncontrolled mental illness — and of finding hope and purpose on the other side.

"So, where lies the difference between sanity and (in)sanity? Well, it turns out, it's all a matter of perspective."

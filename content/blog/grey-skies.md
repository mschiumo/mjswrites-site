not even the gloom of melancholy skies could suppress my jubilance on this day  
for i know just to be alive below them is fortuitous beyond words  
i'll look to them with shining optimism  
hopeful that tomorrow will be accompanied by sunlight  
but mindful and grateful for the chance to experience life just the same
